Automobile Dashboard (AutoLens) — Power BI

Overview
This project presents an interactive Automobile Dashboard built using Power BI. It analyzes sales, profit, and vehicle performance across brands, models, vehicle types, countries, and time periods to generate actionable business insights.
Objectives

Track overall business performance using key KPIs
Analyze sales distribution across car brands and models
Identify trends in monthly and yearly sales
Understand vehicle type preferences and regional sales patterns

Tools & Technologies

Power BI
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
Data Modeling

Key Metrics (KPIs)

Total Sales: 32M
Total Profit: 10.24M
Profit Margin: 32.31%
Avg. Sale Price: 2.64M

Dashboard Features
1. Brands Total Sales

Compares total sales across all car manufacturers
Top brand: Aston Martin (10.7M)

2. Total Sales by Model

Breaks down sales at the model level
Top model: DB9 (5.4M)

3. Monthly Sales Trend (2012–2015)

Tracks performance over time
Helps identify seasonality and growth patterns

4. Sales by Vehicle Type

Compares Convertible vs Saloon
Saloon leads with 17M vs Convertible 14M

5. Total Sales by Model (Treemap)

Visual breakdown of all models by sales volume

6. Sales by Country (Map)

Regional comparison across UK, France, Switzerland, USA

7. Interactive Filters

Make | Country | Vehicle Type | Reporting Year | Color

Key Insights

Aston Martin is the top-selling brand
DB9 is the highest-selling model
Saloon vehicle type dominates overall sales
Sales show significant growth from 2012 to 2015
United Kingdom and France are the primary sales markets
